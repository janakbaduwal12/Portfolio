About Me

My name is Janak Baduwal, and I am a passionate web designer from Chaukune-1, Surkhet, Nepal, currently living in Birendranagar, Surkhet. I am 19 years old, born on April 21, 2006. I have successfully completed my Diploma in Computer Engineering, and at the same time, I am continuing my education by studying in Class 12. Ever since I was young, I have been deeply interested in technology, design, and creativity, which led me to choose web designing as my career path. I love creating clean, modern, and user-friendly websites that not only look good but also deliver a great experience to the users. Apart from my professional interests, I have a strong passion for sports, especially cricket, which is my favorite game. Cricket has taught me important life lessons like teamwork, discipline, and patience, which I also apply in my professional work. I believe in continuous learning and self-improvement, and I am always looking for opportunities to grow and enhance my skills. Through hard work, dedication, and creativity, I aim to build a successful career in the field of web development and technology.
